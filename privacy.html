Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Place2Be ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Place2Be - Nightlife mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile information.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your precise location to show you nearby bars, clubs, and nightlife venues. Location data is only collected while the App is in use.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as venues viewed, searches performed, and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the App experience, including showing nearby venues and events.
  • Manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • Send you relevant notifications about events and promotions near you (with your consent).
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance and features.
  • Ensure the safety and security of our services.

3. Location Data

Place2Be uses your device's location services to display nightlife venues and events near you. You can enable or disable location access at any time through your device settings. When location access is disabled, some features of the App may not function properly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the App (e.g., Firebase for authentication and data storage, RevenueCat for subscription management).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking at any time.
  • Opt out of promotional notifications.

7. Children's Privacy

Place2Be is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
